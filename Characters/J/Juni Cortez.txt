Juni Cortez
Spy Kids
Juni Rocket Racer Rebel Cortez is one of the main protagonists of the Spy Kids series. He is an agent of the OSS's Spy Kids Division, the son of Gregorio and Ingrid Cortez, and the younger brother of Carmen Cortez.
